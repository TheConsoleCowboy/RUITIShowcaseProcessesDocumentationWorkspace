17 February 2023

Following about a week of preparation for the career fair (including resume preparation, both independently and at the drop-in clinic), I attended the fair itself. I will be completely transparent in admitting the fair to be a bit overwhelming, likely for two reasons: (1) being that my ambitions are not necessarily towards industry, I was considerably underprepared in terms of what I was looking to get out of the fair which (2) only really served to fuel my social anxiety when I would approach a table and the representative wsas very clearly not looking for anything other than a candidate they could hire. Perhaps I went into it looking for a network event but instead found a peacocking session.

However, attendance taught me some valuable lessons and have some ideas for how I will reapproach this assignment once I identify another career fair, as I would like to, which I will explain more in another document. Rather than write up the raw, scatterbrained notes containing those ideas, I will instead write them up as two reports:

  • Advice on Approaching a Career Fair; or My Mistakes that You Don't Have to Make
  • The Game Plan for Career Fair Round 2
